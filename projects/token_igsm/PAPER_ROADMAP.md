# Token-level iGSM paper roadmap

1. Establish matched flat and hierarchical predictors with causal masking and boundary tests.
2. Show whether hierarchy adds abstraction beyond capacity and gradient-scale controls.
3. Diagnose on-support planning, reachability, and recursive latent drift separately.
4. Demonstrate executable token generation without oracle or symbolic candidate information.
5. Scale only after the mechanism survives multiple seeds and faithful long traces.
