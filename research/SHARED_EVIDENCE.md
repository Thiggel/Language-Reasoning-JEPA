# Shared evidence promoted across projects

Only findings listed here may be loaded as cross-project priors by a project director.

- Continuous latent planners can exploit off-support action codes; empirical-support and reachability diagnostics are useful across projects. This is a design warning, not evidence that any particular remedy works in all three projects.
- Recursive latent rollout drift should be measured separately from one-step prediction error.
- Failed jobs that never entered optimization are process failures, not scientific negative results.
